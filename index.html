Our Collection

About Us

SkySet is a leading textile solutions provider in Uzbekistan, delivering premium products to major enterprises and nationwide markets. We specialize in high-quality aviation kits and uniforms, offering full-service production for airlines, hotels, trains, cruises, and more. From bespoke uniforms to polo shirts, toothbrushes, pastes, slippers, and personal care accessories — we cover every detail. Our versatile manufacturing allows us to design and deliver tailor-made collections for any industry or need. SkySet is your trusted partner in crafting comfort, professionalism, and luxury — wherever your journey begins.
